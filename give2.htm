    A contribution to TTMB helps support our program operation, development, and expansion.  Help make a profound difference in a young person’s life by giving of your treasure.  Consult the list below for ways to give and submit your donation today.

    Ways to Give

    Credit Card – Give a gift online. TTMB accepts Mastercard, Visa, and American Express.

    Check – Make all checks payable to Emmanuel CEDC –Teens That Mean Business and mail to:

    TEENS THAT MEAN BUSINESS
    C/O Emmanuel CEDC
    36 St. James Place
    Brooklyn, NY 11205

    Matching Gifts – Many corporations match donations to non-profits, doubling your support. Contact your company’s HR department for details.

    Gift Planning – Options such as charitable gift annuities, deferred gift annuities, living trusts, and wills are ways to support TTMB.

    Endowment Gifts – A pledged gift of $10,000 or more allows a donor to name a scholarship.

    VOLUNTEER

    Share your time and talent with TTMB! Volunteers are needed to facilitate work-shops, serve on career panels, host career discovery excursions, and assist with fundraising and outreach efforts. For more information about volunteer opportunities, contact TTMB via email at info@teensmeanbusiness.com.
